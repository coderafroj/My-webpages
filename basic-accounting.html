ðŸ“Œ Ledger in Accounting

Ledger ek book ya system hai jisme sabhi transactions record kiye jate hain, jo specific accounts se related hote hain.

ðŸ”¹ Importance of Ledger

  • Business ki financial position track karne me madad karta hai.
  • Sabhi transactions systematically record hote hain.
  • Trial Balance aur Final Accounts banane ke liye zaroori hota hai.

ðŸ”¹ Types of Ledger

  • General Ledger: Sabhi accounts ka complete record hota hai.
  • Debtors Ledger (Sales Ledger): Customers se related transactions hoti hain.
  • Creditors Ledger (Purchase Ledger): Suppliers se related transactions hoti hain.

ðŸ”¹ Groups in Tally Prime

Tally Prime me predefined aur user-defined groups hote hain jo transactions ko better manage karne me madad karte hain.

  • Primary Groups: Predefined hote hain, jaise **Assets, Liabilities, Capital, Revenue, aur Expenses**.
  • Sub-Groups: Primary Groups ke andar aate hain, jaise **Fixed Assets, Current Assets, Sundry Debtors, Sundry Creditors**.

ðŸ”¹ How to Create a Group in Tally Prime?

  1. Tally Prime open karein aur Gateway of Tally me jayein.
  2. Accounts Info option select karein.
  3. Groups me jayein aur "Create" select karein.
  4. Group ka naam enter karein aur relevant primary group select karein.
  5. Save karein aur exit karein.
